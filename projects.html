LiftOff

DOWNLOAD LIFTOFF HERE
A dynamic mobile app built in React Native. What started as a small project in my mobile dev class, has since become what I have worked hardest and most on. LiftOff is all about motivation and quantitive data. You can track your workouts by specifying the type of workout, the weight, and number of reps. By submitting this data, you are subject to reciving achievements like little tokens to incentivize continuing to track. I use FireBase for data management.

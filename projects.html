LiftOff

A dynamic mobile app built in React Native. This was my first real group project. LiftOff is all about motivation and quantitive data. You can track your workouts by specifying the type of workout, the weight, and number of reps. By submitting this data, you are subject to reciving "rewards" like little tokens to incentivize continuing to track. We use FireBase for data management.
